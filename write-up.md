# Design Rationale

This reflection tree is based on three psychological axes:

1. Locus of Control (Rotter, 1954)
Questions identify whether the employee attributes outcomes to their actions or external circumstances.

2. Contribution vs Entitlement (Organ, 1988)
Questions explore whether the employee focuses on contributing value or expecting rewards.

3. Radius of Concern (Maslow, 1969)
Questions encourage perspective beyond personal challenges toward team and customer impact.

Design decisions:
- Each axis contains 2 questions and 1 reflection
- Options are balanced and non-judgmental
- Reflection statements avoid moral tone
- Deterministic signals track orientation trends
- Bridge nodes ensure smooth conversational flow

Tradeoffs:
- Limited depth to maintain usability after work hours
- Simple language to reduce cognitive load
- Balanced options to prevent bias

Future improvements:
- Add deeper branching per axis
- Personalize summary using signal counts
- Add domain-specific workplace examples
